What I am going to demonstrate on this page

This is my first web page. On this page I am going to demonstrate several different types of things. I will have a banner header and footer, a second and third header, a paragraph, a side-bar, and a thumbnails of several pictures that enlarge when you scroll over them. I will also have a background behind the main page and the main page will look like it is raised off the background. The page will resize based on the size of the window, to a point.
